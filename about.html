ABOUT WAHU PHOTOGRAPHY

Authenticity is key in photography because it allows us to capture the true essence of a moment and convey the emotions and feelings that are present.


I have been on an angelic journey for a few years now, and it has moved my spirit. My need to take pictures no longer affected me, I started to think, as my perspective on life changed. I recently realized that I might possibly have internal reasons for loving photography.


I used to shoot photos in an effort to slow down time. Instead of living in the present, I felt compelled to hold desperately to former memories. In the end, I realized that I needed to shoot images to honor the vibrancy of the present moment rather than to look back. We only have what we have right now.
